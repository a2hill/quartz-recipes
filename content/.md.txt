# Description
This garlic oil is perfect for adding a burst of flavor to any dish. It's easy to make and can be stored in the refrigerator for a day or two.

---

# Ingredients
- [ ] 1½ teaspoons (5 grams) minced garlic
- [ ] ¼ cup (55 grams) extra virgin olive oil

---

# Method
1. Combine the garlic and oil in a small bowl.
2. Cover and store in the refrigerator for a day or two.
